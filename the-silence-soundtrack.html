The Silent God Awakens

Based on Chapter 1: The Unspoken God

Chapter 1: The Unspoken God

In a world scarred by ancient divine war, where spilled god-blood birthed monsters and transformed mortals into the powerful yet cursed Bloodmarked, an ominous silence descends—not mere quiet, but a suffocating presence from the void itself. The Silent God, a deity older than the fallen pantheon, whose name was erased from sacred texts, awakens. Unlike gods of thunder or fire, this entity commands the silence before creation and the stillness after destruction. Across kingdoms, five souls hear tailored whispers blooming in their minds—Accept, Emerge, Burn, Flee, Blind—each word a key turning in their consciousness, binding them unknowingly to the Silent God's design. The age of loud, warring gods has ended; the age of the unspoken God dawns, and the world holds its breath before the terrifying potential of a deity who needs no voice to command, only silence to conquer.
