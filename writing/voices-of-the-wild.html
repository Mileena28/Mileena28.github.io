The Story

In a world where the gods have gone silent, amateur scholar Alaquinn Althaea sets out to study the nature spirits that remain. What begins as simple research into a mischievous water sprite quickly unravels into something far more sinister when the creature whispers a chilling warning: "The Silence... It's coming."

Armed with little more than an expensive journal and stubborn curiosity, Quinn must navigate bureaucratic obstacles, budding romance, and her own inexperience to uncover a supernatural crisis that's causing the cosmic balance to tip dangerously—making nature spirits forget their ancient purposes and act against their very nature.

Told through Quinn's research journal as she stumbles from academic disaster to dangerous discovery.
