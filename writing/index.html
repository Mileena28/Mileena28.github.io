Blood of the Wild Gods: The Silence

Dark Fantasy • Epic Fantasy

Long ago, the gods waged war across the heavens, leaving scars that reshaped the world. Their blood birthed the Beastborn—monstrous creatures of divine wrath. But not all touched by this power became beasts. Some became the Bloodmarked—mortals wielding impossible power, forever teetering on the edge of corruption.

Now, as the Silent God awakens, its whispers reach five mortals chosen for a purpose they do not understand. When silence itself becomes a weapon, the fate of the realm will depend on choices that defy destiny.

Author's Dedication

For the dreamer I once was, and the one I will always be.

To the stories that shaped me, the worlds I escaped to, and the legends that lived in my heart long before I ever put words to my own. As a child, I promised myself that one day, I would write a book—not just for the love of storytelling, but to prove that the magic I believed in was real. That dreams, no matter how distant, are worth chasing.

And now, here I am.

This book is proof that the worlds we imagine can become something real, something lasting. To every dreamer who holds a story in their heart—may you never stop believing in the day it becomes yours to tell.

Blood of the Wild Gods: The Chosen Chronicles

Dark Fantasy • Psychological Horror • Generational Epic

A four-book journey spanning eighteen years, following Mira Roehart from entrapment to empowerment. When a silver-tongued leader arrives promising salvation through Zenith's Light, Mira must navigate religious extremism, discover her void-touched heritage, and ultimately choose when to stop running and start fighting.

The complete saga includes: The Chosen (complete at 22 chapters), The Forsaken (in progress with 16+ chapters), The Village (upcoming), and The Return (upcoming). This isn't a story about quick revenge or easy healing—it's about the long, difficult journey of reclaiming yourself after systematic destruction.

Blood of the Wild Gods: The Lost Histories

Six forgotten tales of divine tragedy and mortal courage. Stories whispered between stone and silence, revealing why the gods fell silent and what price they paid for loving mortals. Each tale stands alone yet weaves into the greater tapestry of the Blood of the Wild Gods universe.

From "Seven Days of Sun"—a god cursed to watch his beloved die through endless incarnations—to stories of the first Bloodmarked and the origin of divine corruption, these histories explore the moments that shaped the world but were too dangerous to remember.

Blood of the Wild Gods: Voices of the Wild

Academic Fantasy • Epistolary Fiction

An amateur scholar's research journal documenting nature spirits who whisper warnings of The Silence returning. What begins as simple academic curiosity unravels into something far more sinister as patterns emerge that suggest these "harmless" spirits might be symptoms of a much larger threat.

Written in journal format, this story explores the intersection of academic pursuit and cosmic horror, where the very act of seeking knowledge might be hastening the world's undoing. Each entry brings our scholar closer to truths that should have remained buried.

I write the stories that make me uncomfortable, because comfort is the enemy of truth. If my words don't disturb someone into awareness, then I'm not doing my job as a storyteller.

My writing comes from the belief that literature should be more than entertainment - it should be transformation. I'm not interested in stories that let readers stay comfortable in their assumptions. I want to write narratives that force us to examine our own shadows, to question what we think we know about human nature, and to find beauty in the places we're afraid to look.

Every story I write is born from authentic experience or deep emotional truth. I don't create suffering for shock value, but I refuse to sanitize the human experience for palatability. My characters are flawed, complex, and real because real people are messy and contradictory and beautiful in their brokenness.
