June 14, 2025 Self-Reflection

On Being Called "Too Intense"

I've been thinking about what it means when people say I'm "intense." Is that just code for "you make me uncomfortable because you feel things deeply?" I've realized that my emotional intelligence comes from having to develop it for survival...

"Your intensity is actually your superpower, even when it makes others uncomfortable."

Maybe the problem isn't that I'm too much. Maybe the problem is that most people have learned to be too little. They've been conditioned to stay on the surface, to avoid the depths where real growth happens...

"Comfort doesn't create change or awareness."

I have this belief that people should make themselves uncomfortable in order to grow. The discomfort is to prove a point, to force us to confront realities we'd rather ignore...

May 28, 2025 Life Lessons

When Life Creates a Perfect Storm

Homelessness is something I thought I'd never experience, but sometimes life creates a perfect storm that sweeps you away. Three months. Only three months, but it felt like years of being adrift, sleeping in strange places with thin walls where unfamiliar voices echoed through the night...

This experience opened my eyes even more to the quiet, complicated nature of this world. But more than anything, it humbled me. I've learned the value of persistence, the beauty of gratitude, and that sometimes love gets difficult...
