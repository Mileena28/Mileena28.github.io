Dual Calendar Systems

Understand how Arcadia measures time through two distinct calendar systems. The mathematically perfect Arcane Calendar tracks divine rhythms and cosmic cycles, while the practical Common Calendar follows the observable solar year. Each month is named for constellations—some visible in the night sky, others existing only in prophecy.
