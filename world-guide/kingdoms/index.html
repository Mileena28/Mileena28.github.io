Kingdoms & Politics

Where Power Flows Through Divine Blood and Mortal Ambition

Explore the complex political landscape of Arcadia, where supernatural threats have shaped every form of governance. From mighty kingdoms built on divine tragedy to underground resistance movements, discover how mortals organize themselves in a world where gods' emotions can reshape coastlines and cosmic conflicts determine the fate of nations.

Major Kingdoms

The great realms where crowned heads rule over territories shaped by divine intervention and cosmic consequence

Kingdom of Atheria

Maritime Kingdom

Built from the ruins of ancient Quenos, destroyed by divine grief when Elodias lost Senia. Now a prosperous coastal kingdom known as the "breadbasket of the east," where maritime culture and agricultural abundance create a society that has learned to thrive after cosmic tragedy.

Religious-Political Movements

Where faith becomes the foundation of power and divine mandate shapes governmental authority

Zenith's Chosen

A powerful monotheistic organization seeking to replace traditional Wild God worship with absolute submission to Zenith's Light. Their hierarchical theocracy combines spiritual authority with political control through systematic conversion and social engineering.

Study the Movement

Forsaken Resistance Networks

Underground communities of those cast out by Zenith's Chosen, operating alternative governance structures focused on survival and mutual aid. Their hidden sanctuaries and resistance operations challenge absolute religious authority through solidarity and strategic action.

Join the Resistance

Temple Societies

Traditional religious communities organized around service to divine beings, serving as intermediaries between mortal and supernatural realms. These societies maintain official knowledge while often suppressing dangerous truths, operating complex hierarchies based on spiritual development and political loyalty.

Enter the Temple

Tribal Confederations

Societies organized around strength, survival, and adaptation to the harshest environments

Bloodfang Clans

Beastmen societies of the Crimson Tundra organized around strength-based hierarchies and worship of primal violence. Their culture represents survival through embracing rather than avoiding the world's harsh realities, with complex clan systems based on pack hierarchies and seasonal hunts.

Join the Hunt

Ashborn Tribes

Scattered survivors of the Free People, transformed by devastation into nomadic scavengers of the Godscarred Wastes. Their pragmatic society adapted to supernatural threats through fragmented tribal structures, relic hunting, and hard-won knowledge of surviving in divinely corrupted territories.

Walk the Wastes
