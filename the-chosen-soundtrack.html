The Chosen

Original Soundtrack

A 10-Track Narrative Journey from Captivity to Freedom

The Story Through Music

This isn't just a soundtrack—it's the story of The Chosen told through music. Each track follows Mira Roehart's harrowing journey from a free-spirited young woman in a coastal town to a survivor fleeing supernatural forces and psychological destruction.

When charismatic religious leader Lucien Altheris arrives in Rivenglade promising salvation through absolute faith, the town eagerly surrenders to his Light. But Mira sees through the divine facade to the predator beneath. What follows is a descent into coercive control, forced marriage, and systematic destruction—until an ancient power awakens within her, offering one final chance: run or die.

Musical Landscape: Blending Celtic fiddle, tribal drums, Nordic orchestral elements, and haunting vocal lamentations, the soundtrack creates an immersive sonic world where the ancient and modern collide.

The crowd's contempt escalates to mob violence while Lucien watches impassively before warning Mira: "Such is the fate of those who listen to the Nimwe."
